Message From From Principal's desk

Dr. Bhagvansinh J. Solanki
- Principal,

Warm Greetings to all the aspiring students of Shri Bhikhabhai Patel Institute of P.G Studies & Research in Humanities, managed by Charotar Education Society, reaccredited ‘B’ grade by NAAC, grade and recognized by UGC as college with potential. The college established in 1995 Affiliated to Sardar Patel University (Vallabh Vidhyanagar, Gujarat). The college offers under-graduate courses and also offers post graduate Course. The college provides skill development courses like SCOPE, Drama and Dramatics. The institute facilitates a number of extra- curricular activities like NCC, NSS and Sports.

The students are also made aware of their social responsibilities through activities like blood donation, nurturing and planting trees, free eye check-up camps AIDS awareness programmes to state a few.The institutes Alumni Association is vibrant and it is a matter of pride for us for having our students working in reputed offices. The measures initiated by the Management, steps taken by the college administration, the devoted contribution of teaching and non-teaching staff and response and enthusiasm of the students helps to achieve the set goals.

I am proud to be the Principal of an institute which is based on Gandhian principles and contributing towards the betterment of the society. Let’s move ahead in this noble cause of teaching and learning through our diligence, devotion and dedication. Wishing you all the Best.
