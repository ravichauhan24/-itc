HISTORY

Shree Bhikhabhai Patel Institute of P.G Studies & Research in Humanities,

Is managed by Charotar Education Society, Anand, which was established on 16th April, 1916 by the Gem of Charotar Late Shri Motibhai Amin Saheb.

The Institute was Establishment on 15th June 1995, which widely spread in 7.129423 acers. Institute also Affiliated under the U.G.C Recognition under section 2(f) and 12 (B) and Sardar Patel University, located at Vallabh Vidhyanagar, Gujarat.
